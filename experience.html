Sequence-Space Synaesthesia

Sequence-space synaesthesia can cause any type of ordered sequence (days, months, playing cards, etc.) to be experienced visually, in the mind's eye or in the physical space around them.

Tone-Colour Synaesthesia

Some Synaesthetes associate specific colours with the musical scale. In this example, each piano note triggers a different colour to appear. Use your mouse or keyboard to visualise it for yourself! Colour perceptions may appear in the mind's eye or in the physical space around the synaesthete and have varying visual forms from person to person.
